Chicken Alfredo Lasagna

With Rotisserie Chicken and Spinach.

The taste will make up the cooking time.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 medium sized onion, chopped
  • 300 grams fresh baby spinach
  • 9 dl heavy cream
  • 3,5 dl parmesan cheese
  • 4 dl ricotta cheese
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 7 dl shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 15 cooked lasagna plates
  • 7 dl shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Heat up a large skillet to medium-high. Add butter and minced garlic cloves along with chopped onion. Cook until onion becomes translucent.
  2. Add spinach and stir until it wilts.
  3. Add heavy cream, parmesan, ricotta, black pepper, salt and rotisserie chicken.
  4. Simmer up and remove from the heat.
  5. Pick up a large casserole dish. Cover the bottom with a large spoonful of the chicken Alfredo mixture. Followed by lasagna plates. About 5 plates does the trick.
  6. Place up another layer of chicken Alfredo mixture on top of lasagna plates. One-third of the mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Repeat previous step until all 15 lasagna plates are used. Cover the casserole with foil and bake at 180 degrees for 35-40 minutes. Bake uncovered for another 2-3 minutes to brown the cheese. Enjoy!
